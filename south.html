Places to explore in South India

This page gives an idea about the main tourist destinations in South India.


  • Kerala:

    • Kerala, in south India, is often referred to as "God's Own Country" for its unspoiled tropical beauty. This coastal state has a destination for everyone whether it's the beach, mountains, adventure, wildlife, heritage or culture you're interested in.



  • Tamil Nadu:

    • Tamil Nadu is a beautiful state, filled with popular tourist destinations. Apart from visiting these, you can also take part in unique festivals, like the car festival, where huge chariots carrying icons of Gods and Goddesses are pulled around the temple..



  • Karnataka:

    • Expanded from Belgaum in the North to Bangalore in the south, it has something to offer to everybody; from beautiful landscapes to rich cultural heritage, serene beaches and scrumptious food, Karnataka is indeed a world in itself!
