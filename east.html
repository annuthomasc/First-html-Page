Places to explore in Eastern India

This page gives an idea about the main tourist destinations in Eastern India.


  • West Bengal:

    • West Bengal offers a colorful variety of experiences to the tourist. Its capital, Kolkata, is as cosmopolitan a city as any. In close proximity lie the Sundarbans, a UNESCO World Heritage Site which is home to the Royal Bengal Tiger.


    Best time to visit West Bengal: The best time to visit West Bengal is from the months of October to March.


  • Bhubaneswar:

    • Being the ‘Temple Town’ and the capital of Odisha, Bhubaneswar is a beautiful city famous for its architecture grandeur and innumerable temples in varied sizes and designs.


    Best time to visit Bhubaneswar: October to mid-March.


  • Meghalaya:

    • Home to an extraordinary diversity of people, Meghalaya is one of the picturesque tourist destinations of India which serves as a perfect getaway for those seeking peace and tranquillity.


    Best time to visit Meghalaya: Blessed with a moderate climate all throughout the year, tourists can plan a trip to Meghalaya in any season.


  • Assam:

    • Soothing beauty, offbeat tourists attractions, spiritual ambiance, the sacred river, humble people, and zealous culture, Assam boasts plenty of fascinating secrets.


    Best time to visit Assam: November to March
